The harm of darkness and gadgets

Article reading time ~2 minutes

Information flows from all sides, and work and study are often carried out on the computer, it is especially important to take care of productivity. One of the enemies of productivity is improper lighting.

Absolute darkness, at first glance, may seem ideal for concentration. However, studies have shown that the absence of light negatively affects human cognitive function. In the dark, reaction speed decreases, memory deteriorates, motivation decreases and the number of errors increases.

Light from gadgets is an even more insidious enemy. Blue light emitted from smartphones, tablets and computer screens suppresses the production of melatonin, a hormone responsible for sleep. This can cause a person to feel alert even if they are tired, making it difficult for them to fall asleep.
Lack of sleep, in turn, leads to drowsiness, lethargy, decreased concentration and performance.
How to create ideal lighting for work and study?
• Use natural light. If possible, place your desk near a window. If natural light is not enough, use lamps that emit warm light.
• Lighting brightness should be comfortable.
• An hour before bedtime, try not to use gadgets.

Remember: proper lighting is the key to your productivity and well-being.

In addition to the above:
• Take regular breaks from work.
• Ventilate the room.
• Play sports.
• Eat healthy.

By following these simple tips, you can create the most favorable atmosphere for work and study, increase your productivity and maintain your health.
